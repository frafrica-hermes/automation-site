Frequently asked questions  Expand All Collapse All What is generative AI? 
Generative AI, also known as GenAI, is a type of artificial intelligence that creates content by learning from existing data. [GenAI](https://www.servicenow.com/ai/what-is-generative-ai.html) can provide realistic and creative text, images, and other media to transform industries such as entertainment, marketing, and healthcare. 
What’s the difference between GenAI and AI? 
AI is a term that describes all forms of machine learning and intelligent automation, while [ generative AI ](https://www.servicenow.com/ai/what-is-generative-ai.html#how-does-generative-ai-work) is specifically focused on the creation of new content based on—but also capable of going beyond—the datasets it was provided with during its training phase. With the recent advent of widely available chat AIs, most AI interactions for the average user actually involve generative AI. 
What are the types of generative AI? 
Generative AI encompasses several types of content generation. These include text generation, image generation, video and speech generation, and data augmentation. Producing text content requires a different approach than enhancing videos or creating realistic-looking images. As such, [each kind of generative AI](https://www.servicenow.com/ai/what-is-generative-ai.html#what-is-the-connection-between-machine-learning-and-generative-ai) has its own unique characteristics and applications. 
What's the future of generative AI? 
Although generative AI has been around for decades, its recent rise in terms of capability and availability has been meteoric. As this technology [continues to evolve](https://www.servicenow.com/ai/what-is-generative-ai.html#what-are-best-practices-for-generative-ai-in-business), we can anticipate enhanced creativity and innovation across essentially every industry, allowing for more sophisticated and realistic outputs in content generation, design, music composition, visual arts, and more. Generative AI will also continue to drive personalization and customization, creating laser-focused recommendations, personalized experiences, and unique products designed to address individual customer needs.

## Frequently asked questions
### What is SAP GROW?

    
SAP GROW is SAP's entry point to cloud ERP, built on SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition. It delivers a complete, AI-enabled platform for finance, supply chain, and HR, with preconfigured industry best practices embedded from day one.
It’s designed around four core promises: Start fast, simplify with AI, grow with industry depth, and manage predictable costs. SAP GROW helps companies go live in weeks and expand capabilities on their own timeline without rebuilding or re-platforming.
### Who should consider SAP GROW?

    
SAP GROW is designed for companies new to SAP that are looking to modernize on an AI-powered cloud ERP platform. It's a strong fit for businesses outgrowing entry-level systems; organizations seeking to unify finance, supply chain, and HR on a single platform; and companies that want embedded AI delivering value from day one.
SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition—at the core of SAP GROW—offers the most comprehensive localization on the market and can support companies already operating globally, as well as those planning to expand.
### How does SAP GROW support finance, HR, and supply chain?

    
SAP GROW delivers end-to-end capabilities across all three domains. Finance capabilities include order-to-cash, procure-to-pay, record-to-report, and financial management, with AI tools for billing, cash management, and receivables. Supply chain capabilities support manufacturing, logistics, asset management, and warehousing. HR capabilities support hire-to-retire processes, payroll, time tracking, and organizational management, integrated with finance through SAP SuccessFactors. Joule, SAP's AI solution, is available across all three areas.
### What does a migration to SAP GROW look like?

    
Migrating to SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition—the core of SAP GROW—follows the SAP Activate process to streamline deployment. The Digital Discovery Assessment helps preselect scope and accelerate 
SAP GROW Fast offers a predefined cloud ERP scope based on industry best practices delivered by certified partners (or SAP Services and Support) to ensure a predictable deployment in weeks rather than months.​
### What pricing options are available with SAP GROW? 

    
SAP solution packages are tailored to your business and offered through transparent, subscription-based pricing published on SAP.com. Customers can start with the capabilities they need most—such as finance, supply chain, or HR—and extend with modular add-ons over time.
Packages are aligned to a defined scope with embedded best practices, helping customers understand what’s included from the start and avoid unexpected complexity. This modular approach helps customers scale at their own pace, adding new capabilities without a full reimplementation.
### What partner and implementation support is available for SAP GROW?

    
SAP GROW Fast is an offering of implementation services with transparent costs that helps net-new and existing customers go live quickly.​ It offers a predefined cloud ERP scope based on industry best practices, delivered by certified partners (or SAP Services and Support) to ensure a predictable, rapid deployment.​
### How does SAP GROW address security and compliance?

    
SAP GROW runs on SAP's cloud infrastructure with a 99.9% service-level agreement and 24/7 AI-assisted threat and vulnerability management. Security features include tenant isolation, encryption, authentication and access controls, and auditable security practices embedded across SAP development processes.
The platform automatically delivers compliance and regulatory updates that cover tax, statutory reporting, and business-to-government requirements across 60 countries, helping customers stay current without manual intervention. Full details are available on SAP Trust Center.
### What’s the difference between SAP GROW and RISE with SAP?

    
Both are built on SAP S/4HANA Cloud, but they target different customer profiles. SAP GROW is designed for new SAP customers to start with cloud ERP—finance, supply chain, or HR—and to expand flexibly across SAP solutions as they grow. It's supported by subscription packages, tools, and services tailored to growth-focused company needs.​
RISE with SAP can have SAP Cloud ERP or SAP Cloud ERP Private at the core and is aimed at existing SAP customers undergoing a broader business transformation and migrating from on-premises SAP environments.
